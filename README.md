InterQ is a high-performance, community-driven interview preparation platform. It leverages AI-inspired logic to provide realistic, timed interview simulations across 50+ professional roles, helping job seekers master their communication and technical skills.

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **50+ Professional Roles**: Tailored tracks for Tech, Business, Marketing, Healthcare, and Engineering.
- **Dynamic Question Engine**: Generates unique combinations of behavioral (HR) and technical questions.
- **Realistic Simulations**: Integrated timers (1m, 2m, 5m) to mimic high-pressure interview environments.
- **Audio Review System**: Record your responses locally and play them back to identify verbal tics and improve clarity.
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: All audio recordings are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. No data ever leaves your machine.
- **Responsive Design**: Polished UI built with Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion for a premium experience.
